When can I expect my order?

Once your order has been handed over to the carrier for delivery, you will receive a Shipment Confirmation email. 

Delivery will take 2 to 10 business days after the order has shipped. 


Can I have my order delivered to an address other than my home?

During checkout, you may select a shipping address that is different from your billing address. Please ensure that you or an authorized representative is able to receive the delivery. 

For security reasons, we are unable to deliver to a PO box.


Do I have to be available at home to pick up items upon delivery?

To ensure the safe delivery of your products, RazerStore recommends that you or an authorized representative is able to receive the delivery.


I will not be home on the scheduled delivery date.

As our carriers require a signature for delivery, the carrier will leave a calling card if you are not at home. You may then contact the carrier to schedule a new delivery appointment.

Shipping and delivery estimates

The 'Ships' timeframe is an estimate of when the item will prepare for shipment and ship from our warehouse. The items on your order will ship as they become available.

  • Your order is estimated to ship on the next business day. Delivery will depend on your destination.
  • If you have ordered multiple items with different ship readiness dates, you may receive separate shipments.
  • Depending on shipment and delivery conditions, your order may ship when all items are ready to ship.

Undelivered Packages

When the carrier returns an undeliverable package to us, a full refund inclusive of shipping charges is issued. To purchase items that were undeliverable, a new order will have to be placed. Please make sure your address is correct before confirming your order.
